A Little About Me

I'm Rachel Kennedy — a former restaurant manager turned software development student with a passion for creative tech and clean, purposeful design. I bring over a decade of real-world leadership experience into the digital world, where I now focus on building responsive, human-centered websites.

I'm especially passionate about helping local businesses in the Rocky Mountains build their online presence. From trail guides to small shops, I want to use my skills to support the mountain community that shaped me — one website at a time.

My style is playful, nature-inspired, and grounded in real-world problem solving. I love mixing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and emerging AI tools to create projects that are both functional and fun. I don't believe AI will take our jobs — it will help us do better ones.
