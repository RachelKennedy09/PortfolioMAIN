ABOUT TASH SULTANA

BIOGRAPHY

Tash Sultana has never followed the script. The multi-instrumentalist, engineer, producer, singer-songwriter, and entrepreneur has built an extraordinary career on their own terms—one defined by boundless creativity, relentless determination, and an unshakable connection to their craft.

From their early days busking on Melbourne’s streets to commanding global stages and amassing billions of streams, Tash has forged a career built on innovation, authenticity, and undeniable talent.

The story began with a single spark: a guitar gifted by their grandfather at age three. From that moment, Tash’s world became music. Over the years, they taught themselves to play over a dozen instruments, becoming a one-person orchestra whose groundbreaking looping skills set them apart.

They even designed their own loop station with a trusted team to take technical pioneering to a whole new level. By 2016, their debut release, the Notion EP, catapulted them to global stardom—amassing millions of streams and producing multi-platinum singles like Jungle and Notion. Viral live bedroom recordings nearly broke the internet. But it was their first full-length album, Flow State, that truly introduced the world to an artist with a vision like no other. The album featured platinum singles like Mystik and gold-certified hits including Murder to the Mind, Salvation, and Free Mind.

In 2021, Tash’s sophomore album, Terra Firma, marked a new era of growth. Debuting at #1 on the ARIA charts, it paired introspective lyricism with lush, immersive soundscapes. It also earned them the Rolling Stone Australia Award for Best Single with Pretty Lady. Their MTV Unplugged: Live in Melbourne album offered fans an intimate glimpse into their artistry—self-produced, engineered, and mixed entirely at their own Lonely Lands Studio. The performance became the most-watched Unplugged session in the Aus-Pacific region, reaching over 180 million people worldwide.

In 2023, the SUGAR EP captured another side of Tash’s evolution. Named after the emotional effects of ADHD medication (“artificial happiness”), the project explored upbeat sounds and heartfelt lyricism. Tracks like James Dean and Bitter Lovers (featuring BJ The Chicago Kid) blended genre-fluid soundscapes with experimental intent.

Beyond the music, Tash expanded their role as an entrepreneur. They founded Lonely Lands Agency, Lonely Lands Studio, and launched Lonely Lands Liquids and the I Am Me Foundation—projects rooted in independence, authenticity, and support for emerging creatives.

Now, in 2025, Tash embarks on a new chapter with the release of Milk & Honey—a deeply personal single that reflects on their humble beginnings as a looper. The track explores fulfillment, fame, and the peace found in simplicity, all wrapped in Tash’s signature genre-bending style and busker roots.

With a career spanning over a decade, Tash’s accomplishments speak volumes: selling out shows at iconic venues like Red Rocks Amphitheatre (in just five minutes), headlining major festivals, achieving 15 ARIA nominations, and winning the 2023 Rolling Stone Global Artist Award.

Yet for Tash, the journey is far from over. With their drive to push creative boundaries and a growing legacy as an artist, entrepreneur, and mentor, Tash Sultana continues to shape the music industry on their own terms—boldly, authentically, and unapologetically.
